About

More About Me.

Who I am? -- A dreamer and researcher who is determined to pursue knowledge in a finite lifetime. I believe in the road lightened by dreams and the years saturated by struggle. God help those who help themselves.

I’m Yu Liu who was born in Chongqing, grew up in Yichang, and now works at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST). Now, I am affiliated with the IDSM (Intelligence Data Storage and Management) laboratory led by Professor Ke Zhou. I research data science and AI for storage at Computer Science and Technology College. I am very interested in similarity hashing, dark data, cognitive data management, cognitive storage, and AIOps.

I am actively seeking collaborators who share a passion for life and expertise in data science, and cognitive storage. This is an excellent place to enhance your education, build your knowledge, and develop your talents.

Anyone who is an enthusiast for front-ends, especially interested in ExtJs, contact me, please. I am pleased to share my experience and insights with you.
