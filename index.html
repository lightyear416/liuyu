Who I am? -- A dreamer and researcher who is determined to pursuit knowledge in the finite lifetime. I believe in the road lightened by dream and the years saturated by struggle. God help those who help themselves. Working at the present is better than crying over spilled milk. When I was young, I just want to find the shell that belongs to me.

I’m Yu Liu, a young man who was born in Chongqing, grew up in Yichang and now works at Huazhong University of Science and Technology.
