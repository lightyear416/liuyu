About

More About Me.

Who I am? -- A dreamer and researcher who is determined to pursuit knowledge in the finite lifetime. I believe in the road lightened by dream and the years saturated by struggle. God help those who help themselves. Working at the present is better than crying over spilled milk. When I was young, I just want to find the shell that belongs to me.

I’m Yu Liu, a young man who was born in Chongqing, grew up in Yichang and now works at Huazhong University of Science and Technology. Affiliated to the DSIS (Data Storage and Information Service) laboratory led by professor Ke Zhou, I’m carrying out my research on combining AI and storage in Wuhan National Laboratory for Optoelectronics. In the next few years, I will devote myself to applying AI to storage including deep hashing，content semantic storage and intelligent tuning.

If you are passionate about life, interested in programming, mathematics, AI or storage, and hope to make a contribution to the world, please contact me. It’s a good place and great chance to improve your education, accumulate your knowledge and give full play to your talents here.

Anyone who is an enthusiast for front-ends, especially interested in ExtJs, contact me please. I am pleasure to share my experience and insights with you.
