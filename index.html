About

More About Me.

Who I am? -- A dreamer and researcher who is determined to pursue knowledge in a finite lifetime. I believe in the road lightened by dreams and the years saturated by struggle. God help those who help themselves.

I’m Yu Liu who was born in Chongqing, grew up in Yichang, and now works at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST). At present, I am affiliated with the IDSM (Intelligence Data Storage and Management) laboratory led by Professor Ke Zhou. I’m carrying out my research on data science and AI for storage at Computer Science and Technology College. I will devote myself to similarity hashing, dark data, smart storage, and AIOps.

If you are passionate about life, interested in programming, data science, and storage, and hope to make a contribution to the world, please contact me. It’s a good place and a great chance to improve your education, accumulate your knowledge, and give full play to your talents here.

Anyone who is an enthusiast for front-ends, especially interested in ExtJs, contact me, please. I am pleased to share my experience and insights with you.

我
